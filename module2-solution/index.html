ManCity Players

Kevin De Bruyne

Kevin De Bruyne is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Premier League club Manchester City and the Belgian national team.

Sergio Agüero

Sergio Leonel Agüero del Castillo, colloquially known as Kun Agüero, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a striker for Premier League club Manchester City and the Argentine national team. He wears "Kun" on his shirt, a childhood nickname based on the title character from the cartoon Kum-Kum.

David Silva

David Josué Jiménez Silva is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for and captains Premier League club Manchester City. Silva plays mainly as a central or an attacking midfielder but can also play as a winger or second striker.
